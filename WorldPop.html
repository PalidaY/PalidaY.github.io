1.What country/region has the largest population in the world?

Asia has the largest population ,compared to other continents. Moreover, it was growing at the exponential rate.
In Asia, China has the most population which is around 1.45B people in 2021, followed by India with the number of 1.4B people in the same year.
India and China population together is around 60 percent of total population in Asia.

2.Which country is likely to be suffering from over-population in the future?

Many of the world’s small islands or isolated states have large populations for their size.
Macao, Monaco, Singapore, Hong Kong and Gibraltar are the five most densely populated.
Singapore has nearly 8,000 people per square kilometers which is more than 60 times as dense as Thailand.

3.Is the rate of population growth increasing or decreasing?

Although the rate of population growth of some countries are fluctuating, the general trend all around the world is downward.
Therefore, the rate of population growth is decreasing.
