PDE Record

The 'PDE record' project aims to calculate the reported working hours by PDEs (Project Development Engineers) and track job completeness.


Overview

Details

Monthly Analysis

The project comprises three pages: Overview, Details, and Monthly Analysis. The Overview page displays the reported total working hours by project and PDE, as well as the total projects by work type. The Details page allows you to track job completeness and the total working hours for each PDE. The Monthly Analysis page shows users the performance metrics on a month-by-month basis.

Together, these features support better resource allocation, identify potential bottlenecks, and improve overall project outcomes, making the 'PDE record' project an invaluable asset for effective project management.
